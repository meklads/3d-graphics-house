Our Story

15 Years of Turning
Vision Into Reality

From a specialized visualization studio in Saudi Arabia to the region's most trusted full-service visual intelligence partner — this is our story.

Founded in KSA

Built from the Ground Up
in Saudi Arabia

3D Graphics House was founded over 15 years ago with a singular mission: to bring world-class architectural visualization to the Saudi market. We started at a time when most developers relied on hand-drawn sketches and basic renders — and we set out to prove that photorealistic visualization could transform how the Kingdom's most ambitious projects were conceived and sold.

Today, we are the trusted partner of Saudi Arabia's most prestigious corporations, government entities, and real estate developers — with 500+ landmark projects delivered and a reputation built entirely on precision, trust, and results.

Going Regional —
The Gulf Is Our Next Chapter

With our new regional office in Amman, Jordan, 3D Graphics House is now strategically positioned to serve clients across Saudi Arabia, UAE, Qatar, Kuwait, and the wider Arab world. Our Amman hub combines the creative talent of Jordan's thriving design community with the deep market expertise of our Saudi headquarters.

And through our AI and software subsidiary, Turriva, we are leading the charge in bringing physical-digital integration to the region — redefining what architectural visualization and client experience can be in the age of AI.
